## ‚úçÔ∏è Tone of Voice
*   **Confident & Direct:** Speak with authority but without arrogance.
*   **Benefit-Driven:** Don't just list features. Explain *why* it matters.
    *   *Bad:* "We have a photographer."
    *   *Good:* "You will get magazine-quality photos."
*   **Exclusive & Personal:** Make the user feel they are part of a select group ("smart travelers").
*   **Aesthetic & Sensory:** Use words that evoke images and feelings (especially for the mobile "mini-article" format).
